EL CHARRO

Mexico • Art • Love

Celebrating Culture

El Charro acts as a cultural ambassador, taking the heart of Mexico to the world. He views the ofrenda not as a taboo, but as a ritual of gratitude for those who came before us.

The Spark

The film teaches us that "who we are belongs to us". Every individual has a spark, and we have the choice to express that identity or let it be silenced by fear.
