EL CHARRO

A Film Study on Art, Identity, and Culture

"El Charro is three things together: Mexico, art, and love."

Celebrating Culture

El Charro describes himself as a cultural ambassador. He explains that traditions like the Day of the Dead are not scary or taboo, but a ritual of gratitude for those who came before us.

The Individual Spark

The film encourages us to recognize that "who we are belongs to us." El Charro believes everyone has a unique spark, and it is our choice whether to express it to the world.
